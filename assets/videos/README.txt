MCQ Café — Order Kiosk attract-screen ads
==========================================
Drop your marketing/advertising videos here as:

  ad1.mp4
  ad2.mp4

They will auto-play (muted, looping, one after another) on the Standing
Order Tree idle/attract screen. Until you add them, the kiosk shows a
beautiful Vietnam street-food still as a graceful fallback.

Recommended: H.264 .mp4, 1080x1920 (portrait) or 1920x1080 (landscape),
under ~30s each, no audio needed.
